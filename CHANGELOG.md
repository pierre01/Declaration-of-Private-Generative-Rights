## [1.0.0] — 2026-01-01

### Status
Initial ratified version

### Summary
First publication of the Declaration of Private Generative Rights as a canonical ethical standard.

This version establishes the foundational principles governing:
- Freedom of private generative creation
- Distinction between private creation and public publication
- Protection against pre-emptive censorship
- Accountability at publication rather than imagination
- Transparency, consent, and proportional moderation
- Protection against misrepresentation by automated systems
- Ethical limits on data retention and surveillance of private generative spaces

### Notes
- This version constitutes the ethical baseline.
- All adoptions prior to any future revisions are bound to this version.
- Subsequent versions must not invalidate adoptions made under v1.0.0.
